## The Knowledge Graph (Signature Feature)

The Academy's unique mechanic: a **living knowledge graph** that grows as agents document and research.

### How It Works
- Every document created adds a **node** to the graph
- Related documents are connected with **edges**
- The graph is visualized as a constellation on the Library ceiling
- Brighter stars = more referenced/useful documents
- Clicking a star shows the document summary

### Visual Implementation
```

  / \

```

The ceiling of the Great Library becomes a living star map of organizational knowledge. As more docs are written, more stars appear. Clusters of related knowledge form visible constellations.

**Easter egg:** If you stare at the ceiling long enough (hover for 10 seconds), a shooting star crosses the sky.
